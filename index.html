Does Image Freely upload my images?

No. Files are decoded, edited, and exported in your browser.

Does background removal run on a server?

No. The browser downloads a model and runs background removal locally on your device.

Can I still process many images?

Yes. The bulk page supports multiple images, shared output settings, and ZIP downloads.

Does it add a watermark?

No. Downloads are your edited image files without an Image Freely mark.
