Bulk image tools with no upload step

Image Freely Bulk is a free browser batch image compressor, resizer, converter, and metadata cleaner. It handles common searches like bulk image compressor, batch image resize, JPG compressor, PNG to WebP converter, and photo metadata remover without uploads, accounts, watermarks, or gated downloads.

Batch compression

Compress photos for email, websites, forms, marketplaces, Discord, and uploads. Process one file or a batch and download results individually or as a ZIP.

Resize and convert

Resize by maximum edge, exact size, crop, square crop, or percentage. Export JPEG, PNG, WebP, or AVIF when your browser supports the format.

Local privacy cleanup

Images are decoded and re-encoded in your browser, which strips common embedded metadata such as EXIF camera and location tags from exports.

Does Image Freely Bulk upload my images?

No. Files are decoded, processed, and exported in your browser.

Does it add a watermark?

No. Downloads are your processed image files without an Image Freely mark.

Can I download a batch as a ZIP?

Yes. Select multiple images, process them, and download a ZIP archive.

Does it support HEIC?

Only when your browser can decode the HEIC or HEIF file. Unsupported files are shown with an error instead of being uploaded elsewhere.
